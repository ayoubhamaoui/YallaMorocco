Story

AIESEC in Morocco is present in 9 cities around the country and has a history of 28 years of activating leadership among young people. AIESEC in Morocco is part of the largest youth-run organization in the world. Projects

Morocco is a unique country with an amazing historical heritage that reflects in the modern life. While discovering the country, you will be struck by the diversity of the landscapes, going from fine beaches to lush highland valleys and passing by beautiful old cities centers. All of those factors make Morocco into the perfect destination for you to not only discover a beautiful country but also develop your leadership in a challenging and enriching environment and make an impact. We offer unique and diverse projects for you to accomplish that.

Yalla Discover

Discover cities all over Morocco and write articles about the implementation of SDGs you observed. Create social media campaigns to raise awareness about the issues Morocco faces.

Yalla Education

Ensure to children all over Morocco access to quality education by providing them a more diversified education. Achieve this by teaching them your native language, animating workshops about Arts, Music and Theater or by introducing them to IT.

Yalla Environment

Raise awareness about the environment and deliver workshops about environment-friendly solutions in schools. Environment has always been a priority in terms of national decisions with many laws supporting the protection and conservation of it.

Yalla Health

The disabled community in Morocco benefits from very little support, whether from the government or from the civil society. While there are NGOs to support them, they lack volunteers to deliver their activities. Give mental support to disabled children by organizing fun workshops and events.

Yalla Gender Equality

Help the underprivileged women in Morocco to understand their rights, educate and enable them to take up various jobs and impart them with the required skills and knowledge. Create awareness about women empowerment in Morocco.

Yalla Social Entrepreneurship

Develop the entrepreneurship and innovation mindset of the Moroccan youth through sessions delivered in collaboration with volunteers from all around the world. By empower young moroccans to become social entrepreneurs, you will be also tackling social, cultural and environmental issues.
